The set of program states is defined as $\setProgramState = \setHeap \times \setStack$ with
\begin{align*}
	S & \in \setStack      &  & ::= E \cdot S ~|~ \nil                               \\
	E & \in \setStackEntry &  & =~~ \setVarEnv \times \setDFootprint \times \setStmt
\end{align*}
